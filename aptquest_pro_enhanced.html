A shopkeeper marks goods 40% above CP and gives 15% discount. Find profit %.
🤖
AI Tutor · Instant explanation
MP = 140% of CP. After 15% discount:
SP = 140 × 0.85 = 119% of CP. Profit = 19%.

⚡ Shortcut: net% = a + b + ab/100 → 40 – 15 + (40×–15)/100 = 19%
